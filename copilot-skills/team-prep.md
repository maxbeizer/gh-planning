# Team & 1-1 Prep Skill

Team dashboards, 1-1 preparation, and team health metrics.

## Usage
- "Prep me for my 1-1 with sarah"
- "How's the team doing?"
- "Team pulse for the last month"
- "Who's blocked?"

## Tools
```bash
gh planning team --json
gh planning team --quiet --json
gh planning prep {handle} --json
gh planning pulse --json
```
